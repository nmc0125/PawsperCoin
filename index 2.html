"Hold on for Purrsperity!"

Key Features

  • Community-Driven: Engage in decision-making through voting mechanisms for charity donations and feature development.
  • Limited Supply: Total supply of 100 billion tokens.
  • NFT Integration: Own unique cat NFTs with various traits and rarities.
  • Charity Partnerships: A portion of transaction fees supports animal shelters.
  • Gamification: Participate in a play-to-earn game featuring cat avatars and fun challenges.
  • Deflationary Mechanism: A 2% burn rate per transaction reduces supply over time.
